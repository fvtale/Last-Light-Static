Last Light Static
A Flipper Zero stealth-puzzle-dialogue game in C.

Tone
Realistic civic noir. The future dystopia is boring: plazas, courthouses, tunnels, records rooms, city hall, queues, cameras, staffers, and municipal pressure.

Premise
You are part of RESISTANCE, a loose network of workers and specialists trying to expose a political-criminal takeover. A mission that begins as a cure retrieval becomes a trap centered on the Crosslink Module.

Core loop
Sneak through civic spaces.

Read people and timing.

Use RFID, camera loops, and social engineering.

Carry evidence.

Deliver it before the window closes.

Characters
Ashley (Ash): support and exposition.

Brick: protest trigger.

Eric: tech foil and betrayer.

Dylan: senior support and planning lead.

Current slice goals
20 rooms.

30-minute mission clock.

Brief top-banner time flashes every 2 minutes.

Stealth, dialogue, and puzzle play over combat.

Early combat death tutorial.

Time-loss capture state.

Multiple endings including best, compromised, failed expose, sellout, sacrifice, and timeout.
